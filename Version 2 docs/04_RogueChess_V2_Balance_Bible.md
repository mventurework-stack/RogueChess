# Rogue Chess V2 -- Balance Bible

## Purpose

This document defines how balance decisions are made.

## Goals

-   No mandatory units.
-   No banned army compositions.
-   Balance through statistics, not restrictions.
-   Every army archetype should have exploitable strengths and
    weaknesses.

## Target Match

-   15--25 minutes
-   Strategic over tactical randomness
-   Positioning determines winners more than card luck.

## Balance Process

1.  Identify dominant strategies.
2.  Adjust the smallest possible statistic.
3.  Never solve balance by restricting player choice.
4.  Retest against all major archetypes.

## Metrics

-   No archetype should consistently dominate equally skilled opponents.
-   Every unit should have a meaningful purpose.
